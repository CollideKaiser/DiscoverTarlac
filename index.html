Welcome to Tarlac

About Tarlac

Tarlac is a province in the Philippines known for its rich history and beautiful landscapes. It's a place where history, culture, and nature come together to offer a unique experience.

Tourist Attractions

Mt. Pinatubo

Famous for its breathtaking crater lake, Mt. Pinatubo is a must-visit for adventure seekers.

Monasterio de Tarlac

This spiritual retreat offers serene views and a chance for peaceful reflection.

Aquino Center

Learn about the rich political history of the Philippines at this dedicated museum.

Capas National Shrine

memorial to Allied soldiers who were interned at Camp O'Donnell at the end of the Bataan Death March during the Second World War.

Kart City Tarlac

A fun place for you and your friends on all occasions. Enjoy activities like Karaoke and Kart racing.

Culture & Festivals

Tarlac is home to a variety of cultural festivals that celebrate its rich heritage, including the Malatarlak Festival and the Kaisa Festival.
